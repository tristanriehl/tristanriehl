About Me

I was born and raised in San Francisco. I am a high school student with 13 years of guitar experience and also play the piano. Furthermore, I specialize in fingerstyle and percussive guitar. In addition, I take classical guitar lessons from Lyle Sheffler.

My inspirations are Tommy Emmanuel and Marcin. Listening and practicing their music taught me new techniques for their unique styles of guitar. They also make really great music.
